### 计算对象

- λ

``` Scheme
(DEFINE (CONS X Y)
  (lambda (M) (M X Y)))
(DEFINE (CAR X)
  (x (lambda(A D) A)))
(DEFINE (CDR X)
  (x (lambda(A D) D)))
```

-->

```
(CAR (CONS 35 47))
(CAR (lambda (M) (M 35 47)))
((lambda (M) (M 35 47)) (lambda(A D) A)))
((lambda(A D) A) 35 47)
35
```
